Expert Accounting & Audit Services in India

Established in 1994 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Kaushik S. Sheth & Co., Chartered Accountants, has been providing professional financial and advisory services for nearly 30 years.

We are committed to integrity, transparency, and excellence, serving clients across Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Kalol, Mehsana, Anand, and Nadiad.

Our firm is backed by a dedicated team of professionals with extensive expertise in taxation, audits, compliance, and consultancy services. Led by CA Kaushik Sheth, we prioritize delivering efficient, reliable, and client-focused solutions.
