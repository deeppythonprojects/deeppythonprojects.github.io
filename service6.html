Audit and Assurance Services

Comprehensive Audit & Assurance Services for Over 30 Years

For over 30 years, our specialized audit team has been entrusted to provide a comprehensive range of Audit and Assurance Services to our clients. Our partner CA Sarang Dave is DISA Qualified and specializes in Information System Audits, while CA Kaushik Sheth is DIRM Qualified and specializes in Insurance Risk Management Advisory Services. Our audit approach is customized to apply the best practices and accordingly suit the client needs.

Our Audit and Assurance Services Include

  • Statutory Audit – Companies
    Statutory audit, also known as external audit, is a type of audit mandated by specific statutes or laws to ensure that the financial statements of the entities represent a true and fair view of its state of affairs and profitability. Governed by the Companies Act, 2013 and the Companies (Audit and Auditors) Rules, 2014. The scope of the statutory audit mainly depends upon the guidelines and regulations set by the government. The financial statements and the accounts of the organization are scrutinized to avoid discrepancies or any possible misrepresentation and ensure reliability. Our audit team is well-versed in Indian Accounting Standards (Ind-AS) and International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS). We ensure compliance with government regulations for credible, precise, and reliable audit reports.
  • Statutory Bank Branch Audit
    Our firm undertakes statutory bank branch audits for various public sector banks. With over 18 years of experience, we are fully equipped with the knowledge, experience, and infrastructure to perform this audit function with utmost accuracy.
  • Tax Audit
    Tax audit is an independent review of the books of accounts of an entity, required under the Income Tax Act, 1961. Our audit team has decades of experience handling tax audits for companies of all sizes, from small enterprises to large corporations, ensuring transparent filing of tax returns and compliance with tax laws.
  • Other Audits
    In addition to the audits mentioned above, our firm also provides statutory audit services for charitable trusts and NGOs. We also conduct internal audit assignments to provide clients with insights that help improve organizational operations and performance management.
