Indirect Tax (GST, Customs & DGFT)

Support for Indirect Tax Compliance

Our team combines deep expertise in GST, Customs, and Indirect Tax regulations to deliver comprehensive compliance, advisory, and litigation support. We help businesses streamline their indirect tax processes, secure necessary registrations, manage periodic filings, and navigate complex audits with ease. With a strong focus on export incentives, GST refunds, and transaction-based structuring, we ensure that our clients optimize their tax benefits while remaining fully compliant. Our services also extend to representation before tax authorities for assessments and appeals, helping businesses resolve disputes efficiently and protect their interests under the indirect tax regime.

Services

  • Compliance & Support Services under GST and Customs
    Includes GST Registration Services, assistance with registrations of commercial premises, and registration under various Customs schemes like EPCG and MOOWR Scheme. We also handle Periodic GST Returns and GST Audits under the Indirect Tax Regime.
  • Exports and GST Refund Consultancy
    Advisory and assistance related to GST Refunds on exports, including documentation, filing, and follow-up.
  • Litigation & Representation Services
    Support for Assessment & Appeal Proceedings related to GST and Customs matters before relevant authorities.
  • Transaction-based Advisory Services
    Tailored advisory for specific business transactions aimed at achieving tax efficiency under GST and Customs laws.
