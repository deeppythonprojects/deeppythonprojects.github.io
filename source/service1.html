Direct Tax Services

Comprehensive Support for Direct Tax Compliance

We offer comprehensive direct tax services to individuals, MSMEs, and corporates, focusing on ensuring full compliance and efficient tax planning. Our expertise spans income tax return preparation, corporate tax strategy for businesses of all sizes, and specialized support for TDS compliance. We provide end-to-end assistance with tax assessments, appeal proceedings, and representation before authorities. Additionally, our team offers guidance on international taxation, including DTAA and Transfer Pricing compliance, as well as transaction-based tax advisory to optimize tax efficiencies for mergers, acquisitions, and restructuring.

Services

  • Income Tax Compliance & Support Services
    Preparation and filing of Income Tax Returns, ensuring accurate and timely compliance.
  • TDS Advisory, Compliance & Support Services
    End-to-end guidance on Tax Deducted at Source, including filing, deductions, and reconciliations.
  • Corporate Taxation
    Comprehensive services for domestic and multinational companies, including strategic tax planning.
  • Tax Litigation & Representation Services
    Assistance with Assessment & Appeal Proceedings, and representation before tax authorities.
  • International Taxation
    Expertise in DTAA (Double Taxation Avoidance Agreements) and Transfer Pricing Compliance for cross-border transactions.
  • Transaction-based Tax Advisory
    Tailored tax planning and management strategies for specific transactions, mergers, acquisitions, and restructuring.
