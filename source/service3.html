Project Financing

Core Expertise in Project Setup and MSME Support

Our firm’s core service revolves around providing essential support for new project setups, MSME growth, and securing critical financial assistance. This is the cornerstone of what we do, offering comprehensive services like project feasibility studies, optimal structuring, and helping clients navigate financial landscapes to secure term loans, working capital, and machinery financing. We are experts in preparing key documentation, including project appraisal reports and CMA data, and we facilitate seamless liaisoning with banks for working capital renewals and retail loan applications. With our specialized legal drafting services for financial proposals, we ensure that our clients receive robust, customized support in every phase of their growth—from initial planning to securing the necessary capital for success.

Services

  • New Project/Venture Setups & MSME Support
    Assisting New Project Setups, Ventures, Green Field Projects and MSMEs through providing them with Project Feasibility Study Reports, Advising the clients on selecting the most optimum project structure considering various financial, legal and economical factors, Assisting clients to avail various credit facilities from the Banks/Financial Institutions such as Term Loans for the purchase of machinery as well as factory setup, working capital loans.
  • Project Documentation & Appraisal
    Preparation of complete Project Appraisal Reports, CMA Data, Project Profiles, and other bank-specific documentation.
  • Bank Liaison & Representation
    Liaisoning with Banks and representing clients in connection with various financial assistance proposals.
  • Working Capital Renewal Assistance
    Assisting clients in the renewal of Working Capital Facilities like Cash Credit and Overdraft Limits, including complete documentation and liaisoning with banks.
  • Legal Drafting for Financial Proposals
    Providing professional legal drafting services for various financial proposals and related agreements.
  • Retail Loan Assistance
    Helping clients avail Retail Loans such as Loan Against Property, Overdraft Against Property, Business Loans, and Home Loans.
