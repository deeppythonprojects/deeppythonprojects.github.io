About the Firm

Kaushik S. Sheth & Co., Chartered Accountants laid its strong foundation in the year 1994 at Ahmedabad, Gujarat. The firm was originally founded by CA Kaushik Sheth who is the backbone of the firm. He has been working as a practicing Chartered Accountant since 1989 and he is having wide experience in almost every area of professional work. He has maintained certain qualities at a firm level as a whole which include high level of expertise and competence of the team, straightforwardness and transparency that reflects in the way we advocate the issues of our clients at different platforms.

An organization stands firm on the strong pillars of its team.  It is the team that supports and uplifts the organization. Our team brings to the table a great combination of experience, freshness and high qualification.

01.Our Mission

Our mission is to build high quality organization that deliver best in class services to its international as well as domestic clients.

02.Our Values

We endeavour to constantly improve the quality of our services through integrated, dynamic and proactive approach. We create and sustain long term relationships with our clients.

03.Our Vision

We envision developing an organization in future years on the foundation of expertise, excellent, transparency, credibility and commitment.

About the Partners

CA Kaushik Sheth

Founder Partner

CA Kaushik Sheth, the pioneer of the firm, is a true leader. A Chartered Accountant and master's degree holder from Gujarat University, Mr. Kaushik began his professional career in 1989. From the start, he focused on professional excellence through hard work, dedication, and expertise.

Today, his perseverance has transformed a small firm into a well-reputed Chartered Accountants firm in Gujarat. He sets an example for the team at Kaushik S. Sheth & Co. with his broad experience in project financing, direct tax matters, accounting, company law, statutory and bank audits, internal and revenue audits, and more. Currently, he primarily handles project financing, bank audits, legal drafting, and related advisory services.

His dynamic qualities continue to guide the team of Kaushik S. Sheth & Co. in achieving new heights and professional excellence.

CA Sarang Dave

Managing Partner

CA Sarang Dave has been associated with the firm for over 13 years—3 years as an article assistant and 10 years as a Chartered Accountant. Mr. Sarang has also completed DISA (Diploma in Information System Audit), a Post Qualification Course from ICAI.

In the year 2015, he joined the firm as a Chartered Accountant. He has been looking after the Assessment & Appeal Proceedings in Income Tax and GST Matters, Statutory and Tax Audits, Compliance Work, Virtual CFO Services, Outsourced Bookkeeping Services, Company Law Matters, RERA Consultancy, and Government Subsidies related consultancy.

With his commitment towards work and dynamism, he looks forward to directing the team at Kaushik S. Sheth & Co. towards the achievement of new heights.
