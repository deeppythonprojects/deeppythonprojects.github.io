ROC Related Consultancy

Comprehensive ROC Compliance and Company Formation Services

We have a dedicated team to take care of all the ROC related work of our clients. Our wide range of services in this domain starts with Incorporation services of Private Limited and Limited Companies, Formation of Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs), Legal Drafting and Documentation, ROC Compliance filing of Companies and LLPs, and assisting clients in voluntarily striking off their companies.

Services

  • Company & LLP Incorporation
    Providing end-to-end services for the incorporation of new Private Limited Companies and LLPs, including documentation, registration, and compliance.
  • Conversion and Governance Consulting
    Consulting services for conversion of LLPs into Private Limited Companies and advisory on corporate governance practices and structure.
  • Annual Compliance & Legal Filings
    Assisting with MCA Annual Compliance Filings for Companies and LLPs, including filing of petitions with the Regional Director for condonation of delay in statutory submissions.

Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) Consultancy

Comprehensive IPR Registration and Advisory Services

We offer specialized services in the domain of Intellectual Property Rights (IPR), helping clients protect and manage their valuable intellectual assets. Our team provides end-to-end services, from registration of trademarks, copyrights, and designs to representation before the registrar for IPR registration. We also offer expert advisory on the maintenance and renewal of IPRs, ensuring clients stay compliant with evolving regulations.

Services

  • IPR Registration
    Providing comprehensive services for the registration of trademarks, copyrights, and designs, ensuring protection of intellectual assets.
  • IPR Representation
    Offering professional representation before the registrar for efficient processing and registration of intellectual property.
  • IPR Advisory & Maintenance
    Providing expert guidance on the registration, maintenance, and renewal of IPRs, helping clients protect their intellectual property over time.
