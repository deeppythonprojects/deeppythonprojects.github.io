ROC Related Consultancy

Comprehensive ROC Compliance and Company Formation Services

We have a dedicated team to take care of all the ROC related work of our clients. Our wide range of services in this domain starts with Incorporation services of Private Limited and Limited Companies, Formation of Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs), Legal Drafting and Documentation, ROC Compliance filing of Companies and LLPs, and assisting clients in voluntarily striking off their companies.

    Consulting services for conversion of LLPs into Private Limited Companies and advisory on corporate governance practices and structure.

    Assisting with MCA Annual Compliance Filings for Companies and LLPs, including filing of petitions with the Regional Director for condonation of delay in statutory submissions.
