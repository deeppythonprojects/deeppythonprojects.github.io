Subsidy related Consultancy services to MSMEs

Helping MSMEs and Startups Avail Government Incentives

We offer comprehensive services to our MSME Clients as well as Large Manufacturing Units and Start-ups in connection with availment of various incentives and subsidies such as Capital Subsidy, Interest Subsidy, SGST Reimbursement, and so on under Central as well as State Government Policies. We have expertise in providing opinion to our clients in choosing the best suitable business model for new projects as well as expansion projects so as to maximize the subsidy benefits to their unit. Our clients in this domain majorly include all MSME Manufacturing and Service Enterprises, Large Manufacturing Units, Textile Units, and Start-ups.

Services

  • Industry-specific Incentive Advisory
    Advisory services relating to the availability of various industry-wise incentives applicable to our clients under different schemes.
  • Subsidy Application Consulting
    Consulting services in connection with application for subsidy, including file preparation, documentation, and submission of claims with relevant Government Departments.
  • Government Liaison & Certification Support
    Assisting clients with certifications, and co-ordinating and liaising with the Government Departments for smooth processing of subsidy applications.
